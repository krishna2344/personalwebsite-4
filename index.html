** Discover My Journey **

A few years ago, Bob took a journey high above the Earth. As he traveled in an orbital flight, an earlier incarnation of Bob from twenty years prior became his copilot. I wonder what the conversation was. Hard to believe!

Biography

Bob was born and raised in Indianapolis, Indiana between 1964 and 1984. He studied Architecture and Landscape Architecture at Ball State University. He transferred to Arizona State University in 1985 and studied Environmental and Social Psychology. He graduated in 1988 and worked for Valley of the Sun School and Habilitation Center for three years in various positions including direct care, residential specialist and social worker. He married Benita in 1989 and has had three children since: Tegan, Tinnamon and Taliesun. He joined the United States Air Force in 1992. He has travelled the world in 20 years as an intelligence analyst and satellite mission planner. His last assignment brought him to Colorado in which he earned a Masters of Science in Space Systems Operations Management at Websters University in 2011 and retired in 2012. He has worked for both the Internal Revenue Service and Department of Veterans Affairs since 2013. He is presently a full time student working on his second Masters of Science in Website Design and Development at University of Denver.
